The School

Welcome to Purapara High School!

Established in 1970, Purapara High School has been a cornerstone of education in the picturesque locale of Purapara, situated in the heart of Sabaldaha, Khargram, Murshidabad, India. With a rich history spanning over five decades, our institution has consistently strived to provide quality education and holistic development opportunities to students from diverse backgrounds.

Nestled amidst serene surroundings, our school campus offers a conducive environment for academic excellence and personal growth. Our dedicated faculty members are committed to nurturing the intellectual, emotional, and social well-being of every student, fostering a love for learning that extends beyond the classroom. .................
